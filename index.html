Because our equipment is designed and manufactured to meet the distinctive needs of the industry, Cougar is able to saw and drill concrete more economically than most.

  • Horizontal Curb Cutting

    Our machine was designed to cut the tops of curbs off only, eliminating the need to cut, breakout, and repour existing curbs to create new approaches.

  • Core drilling

    Holes to 54" diameter. Again, we have drills to fulfill any need or site condition, consisting of multi speed, electric, hydraulic, and pneumatic drills.

  • Wall Sawing

    Depth to 30". We have the specialized equipment to cut flush with adjacent walls, finish corners with our cornercut diamond chainsaws or freehand diamond chainsaws capable to cutting squares as small as 4-1/2" through up to 24" thick concrete or masonry walls.

  • Wire Sawing

    Sawing depth unlimited. A specialized stainless steel aircraft cable, fitted with diamond beadis looped around the concrete, using pulleys and tension to cut any depth or configuration required.

  • Slab Sawing

    Sawing depth to 30". With our diverse fleet of saws consisting of gas, diesel, propane, electric, hydraulic, pneumatic, and high cycle saws, we are capable of servicing your needs regardless of the site conditions.
